Class: Sound

PIXI.sound.Sound

new PIXI.sound.Sound()

Sound represents a single piece of loaded media. When playing a sound PIXI.sound.IMediaInstance objects are created. Properties such a volume, pause, mute, speed, etc will have an effect on all instances.
